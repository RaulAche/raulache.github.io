Rhythm generally means a "movement marked by the regulated succession of strong and weak elements, or of opposite or different conditions". This general meaning of regular recurrence or pattern in time can apply to a wide variety of cyclical natural phenomena having a periodicity or frequency of anything.
In the performance arts, rhythm is the timing of events; of musical sounds and silences that occur over time. The rhythmic delivery of lyrics is one of the most important elements. Rhythm also refers to visual presentation, as "timed movement through space" and a common language unites rhythm with geometry.
